Critical Issues

Problems that significantly impact ATS parsing

Inconsistent Bullet Points

Your resume uses different bullet point styles and inconsistent formatting, which confuses ATS systems.

Current: Mixed bullet styles (•, -, *, and no bullets)

Should be: Consistent round bullets (•) throughout

High Priority Impact: -15% ATS score

Missing Keywords

Your resume lacks important industry keywords that ATS systems look for when matching to job descriptions.

Missing keywords: "Agile", "Scrum", "CI/CD", "Microservices", "REST API"

Suggestion: Add these terms naturally in your experience descriptions

High Priority Impact: -12% ATS score

Poor Section Headers

Section headers are not properly formatted, making it difficult for ATS to categorize your information.

Current: Creative headers with colors and fonts

Should be: Simple, standard headers like "EXPERIENCE", "EDUCATION", "SKILLS"

High Priority Impact: -10% ATS score

Complex Formatting

Your resume uses tables, columns, and complex formatting that ATS systems cannot parse correctly.

Issues: Two-column layout, tables, text boxes, special characters

Solution: Use single-column layout with standard formatting

High Priority Impact: -8% ATS score

Missing Contact Information

Essential contact details are missing or not properly formatted for ATS parsing.

Missing: LinkedIn profile, professional email format

Required: Name, phone, email, LinkedIn, location

High Priority Impact: -5% ATS score

Minor Issues

Improvements that can enhance ATS performance

File Format

Your resume is saved in a format that may not be optimal for ATS parsing.

Current: PDF with complex formatting

Recommended: Simple .docx or plain text .txt for ATS

Medium Priority Impact: -3% ATS score

Font Choice

Some fonts may not be easily readable by ATS systems.

Avoid: Decorative fonts, script fonts, unusual fonts

Use: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman, Georgia

Low Priority Impact: -2% ATS score

Length Optimization

Resume length could be optimized for better ATS processing.

Current: 2 pages with some redundant content

Ideal: 1 page for <10 years experience, 2 pages max for senior roles

Low Priority Impact: -1% ATS score

Optimization Tips

Actionable advice to improve your ATS score

Immediate Actions

  • Standardize all bullet points to round bullets (•)
  • Add missing keywords: Agile, Scrum, CI/CD, Microservices
  • Use simple section headers: EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, SKILLS
  • Convert to single-column layout
  • Add LinkedIn profile and professional email

Best Practices

  • Save as .docx for ATS submission, PDF for humans
  • Use standard fonts: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman
  • Keep font size between 10-12 points
  • Avoid graphics, images, and special characters
  • Test your resume with online ATS scanners
